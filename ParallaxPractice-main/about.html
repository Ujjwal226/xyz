About Kiley

KM Health & Fitness

"Everthing you need to know is already inside you." - Adidas

Originally from the small town of St. Joseph, Michigan, Kiley has a warm approachable coaching style that attracts clients of all ages.

Kiley devotes her life to helping others overcome personal challenges. She, too, fights her own nearly life long battle with type 1 diabetes.

Kiley has been a successful fitness coach since 1997 at Michigan State University. She continued her path of development as a member of the fitness teams at Lifetime Fitness-Troy, The Lincoln Park Athletic Club- Chicago, The Sports Club LA-Boston, and most recently Equinox in New York City. Kiley is also the founder of her own fitness brand, KM Health & Fitness, and can be hired to coach individuals or groups out of their home or office.

In her time away from the gym Kiley values her family most: Calling her mother in Michigan for a laugh, getting the latest MSU sports updates from her father, and spending priceless time with her husband and two toddlers at their new home in the suburbs just outside of New York City. She is truly blessed to have such an amazing network of support.

Please reach out to Kiley if you are interested in becoming the best possible you. She would be honored to help you reach your goals.
